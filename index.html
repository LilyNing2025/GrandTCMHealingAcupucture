Acupuncture

We use traditional acupuncture to help treat a wide range of conditions, including pain, infertility, headaches, insomnia, anxiety, injuries, hormonal and digestive issues, addiction, mental health, allergies, respiratory problems, post-surgery recovery, nervous system imbalances, and more.

Moxibustion

Moxibustion is a traditional Chinese therapy that involves burning a healing herb (mugwort) near specific acupuncture points to promote circulation and support the body’s natural healing process.

Cupping

Cupping therapy uses gentle suction with special cups placed on the skin to help increase blood flow, relieve muscle tension, and promote overall wellness. Our cupping therapy provides targeted relief for pain and tension, promoting overall relaxation and well-being.

Stone Scrape Therapy

Stone Scrape Therapy, also known as Gua Sha, is a traditional healing technique that involves gently scraping the skin with a smooth stone to improve circulation, release tension, and support detoxification.

Therapeutic Massage

Our acupressure and tuina massage therapies provide targeted relief for pain, stress, and tension, promoting overall relaxation and well-being.
